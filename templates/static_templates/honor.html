COCCs depends upon your motivation to learn, and to do so with honesty. In order to participate in COOCs, you must agree to the “Honour Code” below
By enrolling in a COOC's course, I agree that I will:
  • Complete all quizzes, assignments and any of such assessments on my own, unless collaboration is explicitly permitted.
  • Maintain only one user account, and not let anyone else use my username and/or password.
  • Not engage in any activity that would dishonestly improve my results, or affect the results of others.
  • Not post answers to problems that are being used to assess student performance.
Unless otherwise indicated by the faculty of a COOC's course, learners on COOCs are encouraged to:
  • Collaborate with others on the lecture videos, exercises, homework and labs.
  • Discuss with others on general concepts and materials in each course.
  • Present ideas and written work to fellow COOCs learners or others for comments or criticism.
